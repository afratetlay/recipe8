چکن کورما

Chicken Korma

Chicken Korma Recipe
Serves 4
10 mins to prepare, 20mins to cook
454 calories / serving
Recipe Description

A really rich and creamy chicken korma recipe with tender chicken breast pieces in a mildly spiced curry sauce. You could use chicken thigh fillets as an alternative if you prefer.

Ingredients
  • 4 British chicken breast fillets (around 600g)
  • 2 tbsp sunflower or vegetable oil
  • 40g butter
  • 2 brown onions, coarsely grated or very finely chopped
  • 4 tsp ginger and garlic paste
